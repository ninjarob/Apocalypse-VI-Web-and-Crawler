HIDE
Usage       : hide
Duration    : special (see below)
Level/Class : 1st Bard, 1st Thief, 2nd Ranger, 2nd Anti-Paladin

When you type hide, there is a chance that you hide in the room. If hidden
you can only be &quot;sensed&quot; by the &quot;sense life&quot; spell. Even if sensed your
identity will remain hidden. Hide is broken by pressing return, regardless
of whether you typed a command or not. In addition to this, hide reduces
the desire of an enemy to want to aggressively pursue you by 10%.

Example of usage scenario:
       1. You enter hide as a command and it has been activated.
       2. You remain hidden unless you are attacked by a mobile with the
          ability to see you.
       3. You will continue to remain hidden until you hit the enter key
          in any way, shape, or form.
